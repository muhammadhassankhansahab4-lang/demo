Common Queries

What makes BLDC motors better?

BLDC motors use magnets instead of carbon brushes, reducing friction and energy consumption by up to 60% compared to standard induction motors.

How often should I service my fan?

We recommend a basic cleaning every 3 months and a professional service check every 2 years for optimal performance.

Do you provide international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.
